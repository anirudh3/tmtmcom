Explore Tab

Here, you can explore new music by location (see what others are listening to) or by tag (use acoustic features to see tracks which meet your taste).

Explore by Location

  • Location - You can see in the drop down menu, the regions where users of TMTM are currently locations, including the number of users in a region
  • Select a region and click on Go to see what's hot there! Pretty cool haan? Disclaimer : We don't promote any sort of judgement based on music taste!

Explore by Tag

  • Parameters - These are different acoustic features of a track. For example - speechiness refers to the amount of spoken words in a song.
  • Values - You can choose how much of that parameter you would prefer
  • Type in your favourite track and based on the above settings, you will get back a list of recommended tracks.
