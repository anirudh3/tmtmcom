Hunt Tab

Here, you can hunt down your favourite tracks by various different search parameters.

  • Track - Type in the name of track and take it from there!
  • Artist - Have someone in mind, hunt down their tracks suing this option
  • Playlist - If you have a mood/genre/food/etc, there's probably a playlist for it
  • Genre - Search out tracks by their genres/sub-genres. Keep in mind, Spotify should have that genre listed. So yes, 'potato salad' won't work here.

You won't see all the drop down options listed above if you aren't logged into your Spotify account!
